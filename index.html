Frequently Asked Questions

Is PuzzleForge really free for KDP publishing?
Yes — 100% free with no watermarks, no account, and no restrictions on commercial use. You can publish puzzles generated here on Amazon KDP or anywhere else.
How do I save my puzzles as a PDF for KDP?
Click "Save PDF" after generating. A clean print window opens — in Chrome or Edge choose "Save as PDF" as the printer. The PDF saves at the exact KDP page size you selected.
How does the AI word generation work?
PuzzleForge uses a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. You supply your own API key from a provider like Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude. Your key is stored only in your browser's memory for the current session and is permanently deleted when you close or refresh the tab. You are responsible for your own API usage and costs. If you don't have a key, the built-in word bank works great.
How many puzzles can I generate at once?
Up to 50 unique puzzles per session. All export in one PDF with answer keys at the back — ready for KDP upload.
What KDP page sizes does PuzzleForge support?
8.5×11 inch (standard letter), 6×9 inch (digest — most popular for activity books), and 5×8 inch (small format). Select before generating.
How is PuzzleForge different from BookBolt?
BookBolt is a paid subscription. PuzzleForge is completely free with no subscription and no watermarks. PuzzleForge runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to our servers and your content stays private on your device.
Do I need an account to use PuzzleForge?
No account required. Open the page, configure your puzzle, and generate. No email, no sign-up, no login.
Can I use my own word lists?
Absolutely. Paste any words into the custom word box, one per line or comma-separated. Or choose from 20 built-in categories with 600+ curated words. Mix both if you like.

About PuzzleForge

PuzzleForge is a free, browser-based puzzle generator built for Amazon KDP publishers, educators, and puzzle enthusiasts. It was created as a completely free alternative to paid tools like BookBolt, allowing anyone to create professional-quality puzzle books for print-on-demand publishing without a subscription.

What We Build

PuzzleForge generates six puzzle types — word search, word scramble, crossword, sudoku, hangman, and maze — with full answer keys, bulk generation up to 50 puzzles per session, and export to print-ready PDF or PNG at standard KDP page sizes.

How It Works

Everything runs entirely in your browser. No puzzle data, words, or API keys are ever sent to our servers. When you close the tab, everything is gone. We do not collect, store, or sell any user data.

AI Integration

PuzzleForge offers optional AI-powered word and clue generation through a Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) model. Users supply their own API keys from providers like Google Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic Claude. PuzzleForge never stores or transmits these keys.
